Oh hi! I’m TJ VanToll and this is my blog.

I’m a Web Developer working for Liquid Web in Lansing, MI and I have been making web sites since the web was a thing. I have experience building sites ranging from major applications for Fortune 500 companies to small personal sites and everything in between.

I’m an open source advocate and have made minor contributions to a few open source libraries that I use. This blog itself is open source and if you’d like to dig around the source to see how I’ve made it you’re free to.

Also I go by both TJ and Ted. I do this mostly to confuse people and you can call me by either.
